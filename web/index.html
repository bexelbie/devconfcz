About DevConf.CZ

DevConf.CZ is a free and open, annual, 3-day open-source Fedora Linux and JBoss community conference for Red Hat and community developers, DevOps, testers, and documentation writers, organized to sync, share, and hack on upstream projects with the community in the beautiful city of Brno, Czech Republic.
